Privacy Policy - Kamasutra Guide

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kamasutra Guide ("we", "our", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Kamasutra Guide is designed to work entirely offline on your device. Specifically:

  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device using UserDefaults:

  • Favorite positions: Your saved favorite positions
  • Language preference: Your chosen language (Romanian or English)
  • Music volume: Your preferred background music volume setting

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

4. Third-Party Links

The app contains links to third-party websites (online toy shops) in the "Discover" section. These links open in your device's web browser. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for adults only (17+). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17 years of age.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and no data is transmitted to external servers, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Deleting the app from your device
  • Resetting the app's data through iOS Settings
